Tense (Aluminum Castings)

This includes clean aluminum castings, often derived from automobile components like engine parts, transmission housings, and other automotive castings. These scraps are valuable for foundries and recycling facilities as they can be remelted and cast into new automotive or machinery parts.

TT (Aluminum Sheet)

TT consists of clean, old alloy aluminum sheets made from two or more alloys, free from contaminants. It’s particularly useful for manufacturing companies that repurpose it into new products like panels, roofing sheets, and construction components.

Telic (Mixed Aluminum Turnings)

These are by-products of machining and manufacturing processes where aluminum is cut or shaped. Collected as shavings or turnings, this scrap can be efficiently melted and reused in alloy production.

Aluminum Wheels

Sourced from used vehicles, aluminum wheels are cleaned and processed for recycling. These scraps are valuable in the automotive and transportation industries.

Shredded Aluminum Scrap

Shredded aluminum is produced from end-of-life vehicles and industrial waste. It is processed into smaller, clean fragments, making it easier to melt and use in industries like construction, automotive, and packaging.

Zinc Score

Clean, high-purity zinc die-cast scrap sorted for remelting. Low contamination levels make it ideal for zinc alloy production and galvanizing applications.

Zinc Diecast

Used zinc die-casting parts and components from automotive, hardware and electronics industries. High zinc content suitable for direct remelting into new die-cast products.

Zinc Dross

By-product zinc oxide-rich dross from galvanizing and zinc melting processes. Valuable secondary source for zinc recovery in smelting and chemical industries.
